Abstract— Deaf- hearing communication
barriers still exist in education, the workplace, and social
life. This paper presents AWAAJ, a real- time, AI- based
mobile app for two- way translation between
Indo- Pakistani Sign Language (IPSL) and English text or
voice. The system combines MediaPipe Holistic tracking
and a YOLO- based hand detection module for reliable
gesture recognition in different conditions. AWAAJ also
has an interactive, gamified ISL learning environment
featuring quizzes, progress tracking, dictionary and
games to boost user engagement and retention.
Preliminary user- testing with deaf users shows 82%
accuracy in translation. Built to be deployed in schools
and public services, AWAAJ promotes inclusive
communication and accessible technology innovation.
